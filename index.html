Biography

I am an undergraduate student. I am studying under Professor Fuli Feng in the Lab for Data Science(LDS) at the University of Science and Technology of China. During Summer 2023, I worked as an intern under the guidance of Professor Wei Gao at the University of Pittsburgh.

My current research interests mainly include natural language processing and machine learning. At the LDS lab, I also did some research about AI for medical science/healthcare. When I was an intern at the University of Pittsburgh, my research topic mainly included LLM fine-tuning and generative AI.

I am highly interested in pursuing a PhD in machine learning and related topics. So I am seeking an experienced scholar as my PhD advisor. I have strong brainpower and I am always energetic about AI scientific studies. I believe I deserve your trust! Thanks for your time!
