Biography

I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science @ Texas A&M University. I am studying in the NLP group and my advisor is Dr. Ruihong Huang. Before this, I got a bachelor of Electronic Information Engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China, supervised by Dr. Fuli Feng in the Lab for Data Science(LDS). During Summer 2023, I worked as an intern under the guidance of Dr. Wei Gao at the University of Pittsburgh.

My current research interests mainly include Large Language Model and Natural Language Processing.
